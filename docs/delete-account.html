Delete Your EstateNet Account

Last updated: June 04, 2026

How to Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your account directly within the EstateNet app:

  1. Open the EstateNet app
  2. Go to Profile → Settings
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Type DELETE in the confirmation field
  5. Tap Delete to confirm

Your account will be deleted immediately. You will be signed out and will no longer be able to log in.

Data Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently deleted:

  • Your profile information (name, email, password hash, phone number, profile image)
  • Notification preferences, push tokens, tutorial progress, and legal document acceptances
  • Messages you sent or received
  • Notifications
  • Forum posts, comments, and upvotes
  • Properties you own and all associated units, leases, and payment records
  • Property management assignments you created
  • Tenant identity records
  • Invitations you sent
  • Admin permissions linked to your email

Data Retained

The following data is retained after account deletion for legitimate business and legal compliance purposes:

  • Financial records: Invoices, payment claims, payment claim verifications, and service payments are retained for tax, auditing, and dispute resolution purposes.
  • Audit logs: Records of your past actions on the platform are retained for security and compliance monitoring.
  • Invitation records: Your email address may persist in owner-to-manager invitation records where you were listed as an invited manager.

Retained data is no longer linked to an active account and is not used for any purpose other than those described above.
